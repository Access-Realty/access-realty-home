Home Showings: What to Expect & How to Prepare


To prepare the subject property for showings, the home should be clean, welcoming, and ready to show at any time. Simple steps like tidy surfaces, clear walkways, and good lighting help buyers see the home in its best light.
All showings are coordinated through the ShowingTime app. You will receive a showing request and have the ability to approve or decline it. Once approved, the showing agent is notified and can proceed. Please only decline when absolutely necessary, as declined showings may not be rescheduled.
Access for approved showings is provided through a lockbox on the property. The showing agent will use the lockbox to enter. Please note that you should not be onsite during the showing.
Pets must be removed or secured for every showing. If pets are secured, they should always be placed in the same location that was originally provided, as this information is shared with every showing agent through ShowingTime.
Keeping the home in consistent showing condition helps maximize interest and opportunities. Please remember that you only get one chance to make a first impression. Buyers often spend a short amount of time in the home comparing it to others in the neighborhood, so it is important that the beauty of the home and the features you enjoy most are clearly highlighted during each showing.
